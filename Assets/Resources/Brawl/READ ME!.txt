EN:

The model pack from Brawl Stars by Arvi Kivra.
The pack contains models, textures, and photoshop files of the characters' faces.

The pack consists of parts:
"All Brawlers" - Contains all the characters and their skins.
"Brawl Pass season packs" - Contains season packs from the game updates (convenient if you need to supplement the downloaded files, and not download the entire pack again)

There may be some problems with the use of models, their solution you can see in the folder "Possible problems and solutions".

The package is in development and not all models can be here. About updates and changes of the pack you can read in the "Pack updates".

All models and textures were ripped from the game files.

Suggestions for improvement, bugs of the pack and wishes you can write in dm at the links above.

Have a nice evening!
